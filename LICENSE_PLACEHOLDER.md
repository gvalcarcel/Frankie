## Educational Purpose

Frankie was born as an educational project.

Its main purpose is to serve as a learning platform for systems administration, automation, networking, virtualization, Docker, DevOps, scripting, and Artificial Intelligence applied to infrastructure.

Use, copy, modification, study, and adaptation of the project are expressly permitted for personal, educational, or professional purposes, always respecting the terms of the MIT License.

Students, teachers, and professionals are encouraged to use Frankie as a learning laboratory and as a technical reference.

The project aims to promote technological education and the culture of open software.

## Project Philosophy

Frankie was created to show that a powerful laboratory does not depend on having the best hardware, but on knowledge, curiosity, and the desire to learn.

The project was built by reusing available hardware to demonstrate that even with limited resources it is possible to develop professional infrastructure and high-level learning environments.
